<p><p>The offset printed machine market includes two primary types: sheet-fed and web-fed offset printing presses. Sheet-fed offset presses handle individual sheets of paper, making them ideal for high-quality prints such as brochures and magazines. They offer versatility in paper sizes and types. In contrast, web-fed offset presses use a continuous roll of paper, making them efficient for large volume printing, like newspapers and catalogs. Both types leverage offset printing technology to deliver high-quality, cost-effective printing solutions for various applications.</p></p>
