<p><strong>The ENT Surgical Navigation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Type</li><li>Electromagnetic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ENT Surgical Navigation Systems are categorized into two primary types: Optical and Electromagnetic. Optical systems utilize cameras and reflective markers to track instruments and patient anatomy, providing high accuracy and visualization in real-time. They are particularly effective in well-lit environments. Electromagnetic systems, on the other hand, use magnetic fields to determine the position of surgical instruments relative to the patient. These systems offer greater flexibility and are beneficial in areas with limited visibility, enhancing surgical precision and safety in ENT procedures.</p></p>
