Synthetic APIs are chemically manufactured through laboratory processes, often resulting in smaller, simpler molecules used in various therapeutics. In contrast, biotech APIs are derived from biological sources, utilizing living organisms like bacteria or cells for more complex molecules, often used in advanced therapies such as biopharmaceuticals.</p></p>
