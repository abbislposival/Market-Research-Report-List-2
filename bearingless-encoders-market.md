<p><ul><li>Incremental Type</li><li>Absolute Type</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bearingless encoders are categorized primarily into incremental and absolute types. Incremental encoders generate output signals based on changes in position, allowing for relative position tracking. They are cost-effective and suitable for applications needing simple motion control. Absolute encoders, on the other hand, provide a unique position value at any given time, making them ideal for applications requiring precision, like robotics and CNC machines. The 'other' market encompasses specialized encoders, such as linear or optical variants, catering to niche applications.</p></p>
