<p><p>The managed VPN market is primarily segmented into two types: Remote Access VPN and Site-to-Site VPN. Remote Access VPN enables individual users to securely connect to a private network from remote locations, facilitating safe access to corporate resources. Both types enhance data security and privacy, catering to varying organizational needs for secure connectivity and streamlined communication across dispersed locations.</p></p>
