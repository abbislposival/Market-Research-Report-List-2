<p><p>Flame retardant polypropylene (PP) granules are classified into two main types: halogen type and halogen-free type. Halogen type granules incorporate halogen elements, such as bromine or chlorine, to effectively reduce flammability but may release harmful gases upon combustion. In contrast, halogen-free types utilize alternative materials, offering a safer profile by minimizing toxic emissions while still providing flame resistance. The choice between these types depends on regulatory requirements, safety considerations, and specific application needs in various industries.</p></p>
