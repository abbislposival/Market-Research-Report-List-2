<p><p>The abrasives market comprises three main types: bonded abrasives, coated abrasives, and super abrasives. Bonded abrasives are made from abrasive grains combined with a bonding agent, suitable for grinding and cutting tools. Coated abrasives feature abrasive particles adhered to a flexible backing, ideal for sanding and finishing applications. Super abrasives, such as diamond or cubic boron nitride, offer superior hardness and longevity, making them ideal for precision machining and high-performance grinding tasks. Each type addresses specific industrial needs.</p></p>
