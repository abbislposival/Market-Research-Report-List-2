<p><strong>The IC Packaging Substrate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>WB CSP</li><li>FC BGA</li><li>FC CSP</li><li>PBGA</li><li>SiP</li><li>BOC</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC packaging substrates are essential for connecting integrated circuits to printed circuit boards. Market types include:</p><p>1. **WB CSP** (Wafer-Level Chip Scale Packaging) offers compact designs by packaging chips at the wafer level.</p><p>2. **FC BGA** (Flip Chip Ball Grid Array) enables high-speed performance with better thermal management.</p><p>3. **FC CSP** (Flip Chip Chip Scale Package) ensures a small footprint for high-density applications.</p><p>4. **PBGA** (Plastic Ball Grid Array) is cost-effective for a variety of products.</p><p>5. **SiP** (System in Package) integrates multiple functionalities in one package. </p><p>6. **BOC** (Beyond Organic Substrate) focuses on advanced materials and innovative architectures.</p><p>7. **Other** includes emerging and specialized packaging solutions.</p></p>
