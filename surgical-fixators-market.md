<p><strong>The Surgical Fixators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Fixators (Plates,Screws,Nails,etc)</li><li>External Fixators (Unilateral & Bilateral Fixators,Circular Fixators,Hybrid Fixators,etc)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical fixators market comprises internal and external fixation devices designed to stabilize bone fractures. Internal fixators include plates, screws, and nails that are placed within the body to provide support. External fixators are applied externally and include unilateral and bilateral fixators, which stabilize from one or both sides, as well as circular fixators that use rings and rods for multidimensional stability. Hybrid fixators combine elements of both internal and external systems, offering versatility in treatment options.</p></p>
