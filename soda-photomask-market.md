<p><p>The Soda Photomask market consists of two primary types: Soda Chrome Masks and Soda Emulsion Masks. Soda Chrome Masks utilize a chromium layer on a glass substrate, offering high precision and durability, making them ideal for semiconductor fabrication. In contrast, Soda Emulsion Masks employ a light-sensitive emulsion layer, providing flexibility for various applications, including printed circuit boards. Both types play crucial roles in photolithography processes, enabling accurate patterning in electronics manufacturing while accommodating varying production needs and technologies.</p></p>
