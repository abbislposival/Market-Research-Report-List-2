Medium polarity resins offer versatility, working well with a range of compounds, from slightly polar to nonpolar substances. Polar resins are designed for the selective adsorption of polar compounds, facilitating the purification of highly polar pharmaceuticals and biopharmaceuticals, enhancing drug recovery and purification processes.</p></p>
