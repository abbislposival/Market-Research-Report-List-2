<p><p>The socket weld fittings market encompasses various product types, including elbows, tees, crosses, couplings, and others. Elbows facilitate changes in pipe direction, while tees create branching points for pipeline splits. Crosses enable connections among four pipes, enhancing system versatility. Couplings serve as connectors between two pipe ends, maintaining flow continuity. Other fittings may include reducers and caps, essential for managing pipe sizes and terminating flow. These fittings are crucial in industries requiring robust, leak-proof connections in piping systems.</p></p>
