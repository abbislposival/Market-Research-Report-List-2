<p><p>Checkweighing systems are categorized into two main market types: in-motion and intermittent. In-motion checkweighers continuously monitor the weight of moving products on conveyor systems, providing high-speed inspections for high-volume operations. They are ideal for automated environments where efficiency is crucial. In contrast, intermittent checkweighers pause products briefly to measure their weight, making them suitable for batch processing and applications requiring higher accuracy. Both types enhance quality control and compliance in various industries by ensuring products meet weight specifications.</p></p>
