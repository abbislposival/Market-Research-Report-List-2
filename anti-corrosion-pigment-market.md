<p><p>The anti-corrosion pigment market is primarily categorized into phosphate-based and silica-based types. Phosphate-based pigments utilize metal phosphates to provide excellent corrosion resistance by forming protective barriers on metal surfaces and inhibiting rust formation. In contrast, silica-based pigments leverage the properties of silica to create a durable and weather-resistant coat, enhancing adhesion and reducing moisture permeability. Both types play crucial roles in formulating protective coatings across various industries, including marine, automotive, and construction, to ensure the longevity of metal substrates.</p></p>
