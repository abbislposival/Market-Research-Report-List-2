<p><p>The microplate absorbance reader market comprises two primary types: single-mode and multimode readers. Single-mode microplate absorbance readers are designed specifically for measuring absorbance at a single wavelength, making them ideal for routine assays. In contrast, multimode microplate absorbance readers offer versatility by supporting various detection modes, including absorbance, fluorescence, and luminescence, allowing for a broader range of applications. This adaptability makes multimode readers suitable for comprehensive research and complex experiments in laboratories and clinical settings.</p></p>
