<p><p>The industrial adsorption equipment market is segmented into vapor phase and liquid phase categories. Vapor phase adsorption focuses on removing gaseous contaminants from the air or industrial emissions using adsorbents like activated carbon or zeolites. This method is essential for air quality control. In contrast, liquid phase adsorption targets the treatment of wastewater or industrial liquids, employing similar adsorbents to capture dissolved pollutants. Both sectors are critical for environmental compliance and enhancing operational efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
