<p><ul><li>Line Scan Camera</li><li>Area Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed industrial camera market is primarily divided into two types: line scan cameras and area scan cameras. Line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for inspecting moving objects on conveyor belts, especially in applications like printing and packaging. In contrast, area scan cameras capture entire frames instantly, suitable for static or slow-moving scenes, such as quality control in manufacturing. Both types offer high-speed imaging capabilities, catering to diverse industrial automation needs.</p></p>
