<p><p>The swimming pool treatment chemicals market includes various disinfectants and sanitizers. Bleaching powder is a common solid chlorine release agent, while sodium hypochlorite is a liquid chlorine solution used for effective water disinfection. Liquid chlorine is another versatile option that provides fast-acting sanitization. Trichloroisocyanuric acid (TCCA) is a stabilized chlorine compound that offers sustained chlorine release and is favored for its slow-dissolving properties. Other chemical options may include algaecides, flocculants, and pH balancers, enhancing overall pool maintenance.</p></p>
