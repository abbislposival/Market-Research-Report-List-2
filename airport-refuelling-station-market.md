<p><strong>The Airport Refuelling Station Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tank</li><li>Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airport refueling station market can be categorized into two main types: tank and line markets. Tank markets involve the storage of fuel in large tanks on-site, allowing for bulk refueling operations and better inventory management. In contrast, line markets utilize fuel transfer lines directly from storage terminals to aircraft, facilitating real-time refueling without the need for extensive storage facilities at the airport. Both types serve to ensure efficient and timely fuel delivery to meet the demands of air traffic.</p></p>
