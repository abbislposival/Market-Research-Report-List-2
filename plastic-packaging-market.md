<p><p>The plastic packaging market comprises two main types: rigid and flexible plastic packaging. Rigid plastic packaging includes strong, inflexible containers like bottles, jars, and trays, often used for food, beverages, and consumer goods, providing durability and protection. In contrast, flexible plastic packaging encompasses materials like films and pouches that can bend and conform to contents, commonly used for snacks, perishables, and personal care items. Both types are essential in ensuring product safety, extending shelf life, and enhancing consumer convenience.</p></p>
