<p><p>The automotive airbag textile market is primarily categorized into two types: flat airbag fabric and OPW (Occluded Parallel Weft) airbag fabric. Flat airbag fabric is designed in a single layer, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness for standard airbag applications. In contrast, OPW airbag fabric features a unique weave structure that enhances performance, including better gas retention and quicker inflation. Both types are crucial for ensuring passenger safety and compliance with automotive industry standards, catering to different vehicle safety requirements.</p></p>
