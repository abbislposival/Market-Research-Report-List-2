<p><p>Elastic polyurethane wheels are categorized into two primary types based on their chemical composition: PPG (polypropylene glycol) polyurethane wheels and PTMEG (polytetramethylene ether glycol) polyurethane wheels. PPG wheels are known for their excellent abrasion resistance and versatility, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, PTMEG wheels offer superior elasticity, improved tear resistance, and enhanced durability, making them ideal for high-performance settings. Both types cater to different market demands, providing tailored solutions for specific industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
