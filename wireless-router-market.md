<p><strong>The Wireless Router Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Band Wireless Routers</li><li>Dual Band Wireless Routers</li><li>Tri Band Wireless Routers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless router market comprises three primary types: Single Band, Dual Band, and Tri Band routers. Single Band routers operate on the 2.4 GHz frequency, suitable for basic internet use, but can suffer from congestion. Dual Band routers function on both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, offering improved speed and reduced interference, ideal for streaming and gaming. Tri Band routers add an additional 5 GHz band, enhancing performance for multiple high-demand devices, making them perfect for larger households with heavy usage.</p></p>
