<p><strong>The PVD Coating Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Evaporation Category</li><li>Sputtering Category</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVD coating machines operate primarily in two categories: evaporation and sputtering. </p><p>The evaporation category utilizes thermal energy to vaporize the coating material, which then condenses onto the substrate, creating a thin film. This method is known for producing high-purity coatings and is suitable for various materials.</p><p>In the sputtering category, ions are accelerated toward a target material, ejecting atoms that deposit onto the substrate. This technique offers better uniformity and adhesion, making it ideal for complex shapes and multi-layer coatings. Both methods are vital in various industries, including electronics and optics.</p></p>
